    Fazl-E-Omar!

    Fazl-E-Omar Public School is an English medium, co-educational Secondary School affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi.

Situated amidst the land of looms and lores,a dorned with scenic beauty and enchanting atmosphere, and spread across a lush green environment. Fazl e omar public school Benganaparamba,Koodali ,shines with all its excellence and glory imparting knowledge for generation.


VISION STATEMENT

Our vision is to make available good education to ordinary children and to develop well rounded, confident and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential.


MISSION STATEMENT

To motivate, inspire, encourage and support students by providing a safe, secure and loving enviornment to educate them socially, emotionally and cognitively.


OUR VALUES

Respect,resilience,empathy,reflection seek to promote an educational philosophy based on valuing self, others and the enviornment.


OUR FOCUS

To grow children into responsible citizens by keeping all the values towards community, nation & for enviornment.


OUR PEDAGOGICAL VISION

Child centered and teachers remain only as guide.


OUR TEACHING LEARNING APPROACH

Interactive approach makes class room live & interesting.

Principal's Message

I have great expectations offering the best education for our children. In the modern world, changes are constant. The children are entrusted in our hands and we are to mould them in to great personalities in this ever changing world and equip them with all modern skills.
